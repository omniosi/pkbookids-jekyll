Returns

Your pkboo purchase is made with the highest quality, however if you are not satisfied with your purchase we ask that you follow the steps below within 14 days of receipt.

Please return or exchange items in new, unwashed condition. Complete the return or exchange form included with your purchase. Package items securely and ship via a trackable carrier to the following address:

pkbookids Returns
PO Box 313
Eastchester, NY 10709-0313

We process returns promptly. You should receive a exchange within two weeks. Credit card refunds will appear on our statement within two billing cycles. Original shipping fees are not refundable and we cannot refund shipping charges for return orders.
